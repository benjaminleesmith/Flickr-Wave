Flickr Wave

A Flickr Gadget for Google Wave!

add your latest photos to google wave

To use, simply paste http://flickr-wave.heroku.com/flickr/all into the "Add Gadget by URL" popup on Google Wave.

To specify the number of photos to show, add a number at the end of the url: http://flickr-wave.heroku.com/flickr/all/7

To limit photos from a particular user add a ?user=username: http://flickr-wave.heroku.com/flickr/all/7?user=benjamin.smith
